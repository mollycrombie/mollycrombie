About ‘Renovation’

‘Renovation’ is a project combining poetic narrative with Interactive Fiction (IF) mechanics.

There is no “correct” ending to Renovation. Player can conclude the story at any time resulting in different endings based on their prior actions. As such, it is intended to be played multiple times for a richer experience. Players are encouraged to take their time interacting with the world and examining the effects of their actions in the changing environment.

Unlike many other IF title ‘Renovations’ takes place in a single room, eliminating the need for directional commands.

Tips for New Players

Try typing a verb followed by a noun, then press enter. e.g. 'examine walls'.

If you encounter any italicised words: try hovering over them with your mouse cursor.

Main Commands

EXAMINE | TAKE | PUT (something) ON (something) | TEAR

Single Word Commands

LOOK to look around
INVENTORY to see what you are carrying
END to finish at any time

Single Letter Commands

X = EXAMINE | L = LOOK | I = INVENTORY
